== Installation ==

= Automatic Installation =
* 	Login to your WordPress Admin area
* 	Go to "Plugins > Add New" from the left hand menu
* 	In the search box type __RCOMGateway__
*	From the search result you will see __RCOMGateway by rconsolidate.com__ click on __Install Now__ to install the plugin
*	A popup window will ask you to confirm your wish to install the Plugin.
*	After installation, activate the plugin.
* 	Open the settings page for WooCommerce and click the "Checkout" tab.
* 	Click on the __RC OMBF Gateway__ link from the available Checkout Options
*	Configure your __RC OMBF Gateway__ settings. See below for details.

= Manual Installation =
1. 	Download the plugin zip file
2. 	Login to your WordPress Admin. Click on "Plugins > Add New" from the left hand menu.
3.  Click on the "Upload" option, then click "Choose File" to select the zip file from your computer. Once selected, press "OK" and press the "Install Now" button.
4.  Activate the plugin.
5. 	Open the settings page for WooCommerce and click the "Checkout" tab.
6. 	Click on the __RC OMBF Gateway__ link from the available Checkout Options
7.	Configure your __RC OMBF Gateway__ settings. See below for details.

= Configure the plugin =
To configure the plugin, go to __WooCommerce > Settings__Â from the left hand menu, then click __Checkout__ from the top tab. You will see __RCOMGateway__ as part of the available Checkout Options. Click on it to configure the payment gateway.

* __Enable/Disable__ - check the box to enable RCOMGateway Payment Gateway.
* __Title__ - allows you to determine what your customers will see this payment option as on the checkout page.
* __Description__ - controls the message that appears under the payment fields on the checkout page. Here you can specify that you accept Burkina Faso Orange Money mobile payment.
* __Gateway Key__ - Enter your Gateway Secret Key here. Get your Gateway secret key by contacting customer care at (__support@rconsolidate.com__)
* __Orange Money API MSISDN__ - Enter your Merchant MSISDN here. Get your Merchant MSISDN from your partnership contract with Orange Money Burkina Faso
* __Orange Money API USERNAME__ - Enter your Merchant USERNAME here. Get Get your Merchant USERNAME from your partnership contract with Orange Money Burkina Faso
* __Orange Money API PASSWORD__ - Enter your Merchant PASSWORD here. Get your Get your Merchant PASSWORD from your partnership contract with Orange Money Burkina Faso
* Click on __Save Changes__ for the changes you made to be effected.
